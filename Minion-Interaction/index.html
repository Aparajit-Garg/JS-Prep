Fun Fact

The Minions language is mostly unintelligible or barely intelligible talking with some words from the Spanish, French, Italian, Filipino, and other foreign languages. Many will also likely notice the presence of various words that refer to food. In the Minions song, there’s the banana and potato. The Minions were also heard saying “poulet tiki masala,” which is the French equivalent for “Indian chicken dish.”
